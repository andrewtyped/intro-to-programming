I am a developer, and so can you!

What do you think about software development?
When I was young, there was a certain stigma around programming. I heard messages like:
  • It's very hard.
  • It's tedious.
  • You need a special kind of brain.
  • There's a lot of competition for jobs.
Messages like this may have kept me from investigating programming as a career earlier. They're half-truths.
  • It's very hard (Sometimes. Often it's fun despite being hard.)
  • You need a special kind of brain. (It helps to be logic-, science-, or design-minded. These are teachable)
