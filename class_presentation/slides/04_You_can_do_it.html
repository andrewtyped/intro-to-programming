Programming Stigmas

  • It's very hard.
    • Sometimes. Isn't anything worth doing hard?
  • It's tedious.
    • There are some rote tasks. Balanced by creative / investigative tasks.
  • You need a special kind of brain.
    • It helps to be logic-, research-, or design-minded. These can all be taught.
  • There's a lot of competition for jobs.
    • 17% Growth Rate 2014-2024 (much faster than average)
